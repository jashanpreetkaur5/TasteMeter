Sample Form:

Our form follows a minimalist design with a simple layout, using the Open Sans font. The form fields are outlined in dark purple with a dark pink submit button. The form also includes a checkbox for users to opt-in to our newsletter.

Sample Buttons:

We use hover buttons to provide an interactive user experience. The buttons use a flat design and the Open Sans font in bold. The primary button color is dark pink, while the secondary button color is dark purple. When a user hovers over a button, the color changes to the accent color of light purple.

Spacing:

We use ample spacing throughout our website to provide a clean and easy-to-read layout. The margins and padding are consistent and evenly distributed. The spacing between elements is also consistent, with enough space to ensure a clutter-free design. The images are also properly aligned with sufficient spacing to ensure they don't appear crowded. We also use vector images to ensure that the icons and illustrations maintain their quality and resolution regardless of screen size.
